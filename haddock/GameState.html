isWinFor :: GameState Board Turn -> Player -> Bool Source #

Determine whether Player has achieved the victory condition.

The main diagonal is the sequence \( \{ 0, d+1, \cdots, s-1 \} \). The off diagonal is the sequence \( \{ d-1, 2d-2, \cdots, s-d \} \).
